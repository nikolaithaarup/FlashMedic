## Source hierarchy used in the pilot

- General pharmacokinetics, pharmacodynamics, adverse-reaction and administration concepts: NCBI Bookshelf clinical pharmacology chapters.
- Medication-safety principles: World Health Organization, *Medication Without Harm*.
- EU/Danish additional-monitoring symbol: Lægemiddelstyrelsen.
- Operational treatment, exact dose, contraindication and route decisions: not approved by these general references; these remain `draft` pending current product information and relevant Danish/regional instruction.
